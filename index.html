About CareBridge

CareBridge is an AI-powered healthcare coordination platform designed to solve critical healthcare challenges: OPD overcrowding, diagnostic delays, bed availability, and emergency response.

We integrate advanced AI chatbots for symptom checking, prescription analysis, and emergency guidance, while providing real-time visibility into hospital queues, diagnostic slots, ICU beds, and ambulance tracking - all in one comprehensive platform.

Our AI-powered features include symptom analysis, prescription scanning, emergency routing, and 24/7 medical assistance - making healthcare accessible, efficient, and intelligent for everyone.
